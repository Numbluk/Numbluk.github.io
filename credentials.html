About

My educational background is in Geology to which I credit my patience and ability to endure long stretches of work with the ungodly amount of hours spent staring down the hole of a microscope. Ultimately, I didn't see myself doing lab work for the foreseeable future (I still haven't lost my love for the outdoors though!) and decided to pursue other interests like web development. I enjoy brewing beer when the time allows it and I take poetry very, very seriously.

I consider myself stack-agnostic, but I truly enjoy the back-end (Ruby, Rails, TDD, SQL, etc.) and consider it my strongest suit. Fundamentals are extremely important to me which is why I also consider myself tool-agnostic; my experience with Backbone is only important insofar as my quality of Javascript and JQuery.

I'm looking for a place where I can contribute and grow with a goal-oriented team that takes pride in their work and product and pushes the idea of life-long learning. I also want that team to place great importance on being open to ideas in the workplace.
